Event Venue

RMIT - Storey Hall

Thanks to the generosity of our sponsors, this years event will be hosted at RMIT's wonderful Storey Hall, a short walk from the Melbourne Central train station.

F.A.Q

  • Do I need to pay for a ticket?

    No! Global Azure is organised globally by individuals looking to give back to the Technology community. The event is completely free, sign up at meetup.com

  • How do I get there?

    This years event is located at TBD, a short walk from Flinders Street station.

  • Can I present at the event?

    Yes! If you're interested in presenting, head on over to our Call for Speakers here.

  • Do I need to bring lunch?

    Thanks to our generous sponsors, we have organised a lunch and afternoon tea for attendees.

  • Can my company sponsor this event?

    Yes! If your organisation is interested in sponsoring, please reach our to our organisers via Meetup.com

  • Will sessions be streamed online??

    The 2024 event will be an in person event. Sessions will be recorded and slides collected to attendees to access post event!
